Features

Text fixes that stay out of your way

⚡

Silent Fix

Select text in any app. Press the hotkey. Your clipboard text is fixed and written back — no window, no interruption, no copy-paste loop.

✍️

Deep Enhance

Open the enhancement panel to rewrite for tone, brevity, or formality. Preview the result before it replaces your text.

🤖

BYOK

Bring your own API key — OpenAI, Anthropic, Ollama, or AWS Bedrock. Keys stay in your OS keyring and never leave your machine.

🌍

Multilingual

Writes in English? Fixed in English. German? Fixed in German. KeyLint detects the language and corrects without translating.

🖥️

System Tray

Lives in your system tray. Single-click for the menu, double-click to open. Uses minimal resources.

🔒

Private

All API calls go through the local Go backend on your machine. No telemetry, no cloud sync, no clipboard history stored.
