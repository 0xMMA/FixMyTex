Features

Text fixes that stay out of your way

⚡

Silent hotkey fix

Press a global shortcut and your clipboard text is fixed and replaced — no window focus, no interruption, no copy-paste loop.

✍️

Advanced enhancement

Open the full UI to rewrite for tone, brevity, formality, or a custom instruction. Preview before replacing.

🤖

Your AI, your keys

Connect your own OpenAI, Anthropic, Ollama, or AWS Bedrock account. Keys stay in your OS keyring — never leave your machine.

🌍

Multilingual

Automatically detects the language of your text and fixes it in-language without translating.

🖥️

Lives in your tray

Minimizes to the system tray and stays ready. Single-click for the menu, double-click to open the UI.

🔒

Private by design

All API calls go through the local Go backend. No telemetry, no cloud sync, no clipboard logging.

Works with the AI providers you already use

OpenAI Anthropic Ollama (local) AWS Bedrock

Start fixing text in minutes

Download the latest release, add your API key, and press the hotkey.
